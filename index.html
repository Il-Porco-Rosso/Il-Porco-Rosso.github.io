About us

In the streets of New Pork City, eastward toward the Wellington District, Morris Merrier worked the 9 to 5 in the Orange Co. Office Complex. When he was fired, due to a technical oversight, he found himself on a bus stop where Jean, a long time friend, came to him with an amazing idea. He thought they could take advantage of the increasing orange market, by selling oranges. And thus, Thief Delivery was born.
